CURRENT PROJECT

Vector Art

This is the project that I am currently working on and it's still 70% in progress. Actually, it's not a fun mental place to be in, there's just so much confusion going on. At first, I started with the idea of making a realistic vector girl, but on the process of experimentation I came up with some interesting outcomes. These outcomes are majorly based on the color palette & line weight, but they change the mood completely. It's really interesting how small changes can make such a massive difference in art style.
